Foodie

Foodie is an interactive website designed for culinary enthusiasts who want to explore new recipes and share their own creations. Users can browse a diverse collection of recipes, ranging from appetizers to desserts, and contribute their own recipes for others to enjoy. The site includes user authentication for a personalized experience, allowing members to save favorite recipes, manage their submissions, and interact with the community. With a user-friendly interface powered by Django and styled with Bootstrap 5, Foodie offers an engaging and seamless experience for discovering and sharing culinary delights.
